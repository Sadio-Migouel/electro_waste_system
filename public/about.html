About us

Building a cleaner e-waste journey for residents, admins, and collectors.

E-Waste System connects the people requesting disposal services with the teams responsible for approving, assigning, and collecting electronic waste in a more structured way.

Mission

Make responsible disposal easier to access.

We aim to simplify how communities report and collect unwanted electronics by reducing manual coordination and offering a workflow that is easy to follow.

Vision

Create cities where electronic waste is tracked, recovered, and handled with care.

Our long-term view is a smarter waste ecosystem where collection becomes more predictable, reporting becomes more useful, and environmental harm is reduced.

Objectives

What the platform is built to deliver

  • Provide a simple digital process for pickup requests.
  • Help administrators approve and assign requests without confusion.
  • Support collectors with clear job visibility and status updates.
  • Improve accountability through tracking and reporting.
  • Encourage better disposal habits across the community.
Why e-waste matters

Improper disposal creates lasting environmental risks

  • Discarded devices can leak hazardous substances into soil and water.
  • Informal dumping wastes recoverable materials and reusable parts.
  • Growing device turnover means communities need better collection systems.
  • Public awareness improves when disposal options are visible and convenient.
Our solution

A connected workflow that keeps everyone aligned

Instead of relying on scattered calls, paper notes, or manual follow-up, E-Waste System centralizes requests and status updates in one web experience. The result is better oversight, faster coordination, and a smoother experience for users and staff.

Meet the workflow

From report to recovery in four steps

Users submit requests, admins validate and assign them, and collectors close the loop with collection updates. That structure helps the service remain transparent and manageable as demand grows.
